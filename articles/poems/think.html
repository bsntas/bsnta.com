Poem

Overthink? Perhaps not

Basanta Sharma

Overthink? Perhaps not
But keep thinking
If you hit a dead end
Or a whirlpool bend
Start over and keep thinking
Until you understand it all
Or plan it out to make them fall
Talk it out if you may
Write it down if you will
But think it out for sure
Coz thoughts are just yours
Purely and absolutely
Untainted, not judged

Think without a deadline
Even if it means you hit
Your own life’s deadline
Keep thinking until
You no longer need to think more
And in that thoughtlessness
You shall know yourself
